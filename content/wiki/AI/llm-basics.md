# LLM Basics

Large Language Models (LLMs) are neural networks trained on vast text corpora
to predict and generate human-like text.

## Key Concepts

- **Transformer Architecture**: Attention mechanism, encoder-decoder
- **Pre-training**: Learning from large-scale text data
- **Fine-tuning**: Adapting to specific tasks
- **Inference**: Generating text from prompts
